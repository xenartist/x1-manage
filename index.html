Update Vote Account Commission

Commission must be between 0% and 100%

Commission Information

Commission is the percentage of rewards that the validator keeps. The remaining rewards are distributed to delegators.

  • You need withdraw authority to update commission
  • New commission will apply to future rewards
  • A small transaction fee is required
Important: Changing commission may affect your delegators. Consider communicating the change to your community.

Delegation Information

Delegating your stake account will:

  • Delegate the entire stake account balance to the validator
  • Start earning rewards from the next epoch
  • Require a small transaction fee
  • Lock the stake until you deactivate the delegation
Important: Make sure to verify the vote account address. Once delegated, your stake will be locked until you deactivate the delegation.
